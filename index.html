Frequently Asked Questions

What is the response time for RedMed ambulance services? +
Our average response time is under 15 minutes in urban areas and 20-30 minutes in suburban/rural locations. We prioritize emergencies for fastest response.
What types of ambulances do you provide? +
We offer a range of ambulances including Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Life Support (ALS), ICU ambulances, air ambulances, mortuary vans, and specialized transport for patients with specific needs.
Do you provide intercity ambulance services? +
Yes, we provide both intracity and intercity ambulance services across all the cities we operate in. Our trained paramedics ensure safe patient transport over long distances.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and also work with major insurance providers. Emergency cases are given priority regardless of payment method.
Are your staff trained and certified? +
All our paramedics and drivers are certified professionals with regular training in emergency medical procedures and patient handling. We maintain the highest standards of medical care.
Do you provide ambulance services 24/7? +
Yes, RedMed operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all holidays. Our emergency helpline is always active to respond to your needs.
