Frequently Asked Questions

Get immediate answers to common questions about our ambulance services

What types of ambulances do you provide?

We provide comprehensive ambulance services including Patient Transfer, Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Life Support (ALS), Dead Body Ambulance (Small & Big), Freezer Ambulance, Air Ambulance, and Train Ambulance services. Each ambulance is equipped according to its specific medical requirements.

How quickly can you dispatch an ambulance?

We maintain a rapid response system and aim to dispatch the nearest available ambulance within 5-15 minutes of receiving your booking confirmation. Our GPS-enabled dispatch system ensures the closest ambulance is sent to your location.

Are your staff trained medical professionals?

Yes, all our ambulances are staffed with trained paramedics, emergency medical technicians, and drivers with medical training. Our medical staff undergoes regular training and certification to maintain the highest standards of emergency care.

Do you provide services 24/7?

Yes, we operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all holidays and weekends. Our emergency helpline is always active to handle any medical emergency situation at any time.

What is your service area coverage?

We provide services across all major cities in India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, and 40+ other cities. We're continuously expanding our service network to reach more locations.

How is the payment processed?

We offer multiple payment options including cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and online payments. Payment is typically made after the service is completed. For corporate clients and regular customers, we also provide monthly billing options.
